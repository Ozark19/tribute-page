Gabriel Landeskog

Captain of the Colorado Avalanche

Gabriel Landeskog skates during a break in the action against the Chicago Blackhawks at the Pepsi Center on December 29, 2018.

Highlights of Landeskog's career:


  • June 2011 - Drafted second overall by the Colorado Avalanche

  • June 2012 - Wins the Calder Memorial Trophy

  • Sept 2012 - Made team captain

  • July 2013 - Signs 7 year, $39 million extension

  • April 2014 - First playoff appearance

  • Nov 2017 - Record first career hat trick

  • Feb 2019 - First NHL All-Star Game appearance
